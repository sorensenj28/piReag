Mehlich 3 Protocol (M3):
Stock Solution:
Fill a 100 mL volumetric flask with 50-60 mL of DI Water
Add 5.556 g of Ammonium Flouride (NH4F)
Add 2.823 g of Ethlenediaminetetraacetic acid (EDTA)
Stir on hotplate until dissolved
Dilute to mark.

Working Solution:
1 L:
Fill a 1,000 mL volumetric flask with 500-700 mL of DI Water
Add 50. 025 g of Ammonium Nitrat (NH4NO3)
Add 25 mL of stock solution
Add 28.75 mL of glacial acetic acid
Add 20.5 mL of 10% nitric acid (HNO3)
Stir on hotplate until disssolved
Dilute to mark.

2 L:
Fill a 2,000 mL volumetric flask with 700-800 mL of DI Water
Add 100.05 g of Ammonium Nitrate (NH4NO3)
Add 50 mL of Stock Solution
Add 57.5 mL of glacial acetic acid
Add 41 mL of 10% nitric acid
Stir on hotplate until dissolved
Dilute to mark.


Haney 3 Acids (H3A):
1 L:
Fill a 1,000 mL volumetric flask with 500-700 mL of DI Water
Add 1.1 g of Malic Acid
Add 0.7 g of Citric Acid
Add 0.45 g of Oxalic Acid
Stir on hotplate until dissolved
Dilute to mark.

2 L:
Fill a 2,000 mL volumetric flask with 1,000 to 1,400 mL of DI Water
Add 2.2 g of Malic Acid
Add 1.4 g of Citric Acid
Add 0.90 g of Oxalic Acid
Stir on hotplate until dissolved
Dilute to mark.


Olsen P (0.5 M NaHCO3):
1 L:
Fill a 1,000 mL volumetric flask with 500-700 mL of DI Water
Add 42.0 g of Sodium Carbonate (NaHCO3)
Stir on hotplate until dissolved
Check pH and add Sodium Hydroxide (NaOH) as needed until solution is 8.0
Use Hydrochloric Acid (HCl) if pH is too high
Dilute to mark.

2 L:
Fill a 2,000 mL volumetric flask with 1,000-1,400 mL of DI Water
Add 84.0 g of Sodium Carbonate (NaHCO3)
Stir on hotplate until dissolved
Check pH and add Sodium Hydroxide (NaOH) as needed until solution is 8.0
Use Hydrochloric Acid (HCl) if pH is too high
Dilute to mark.
